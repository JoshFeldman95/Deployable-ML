another detailed work on the subject by dr dwivedi takes us back in time to pre partioned panjab dr dwivedi chose a difficult subject for his movie debut he has worked on all meticulous details to bring the story to survive the treatment of the subject is very delicate br br even though we have not been to the region during that time, the sets and costumes look real although most movies made on partition, this one concentrate not on the gory details of violence to attract audience, but on its after effects the characters come to life priyanshu chatterjee has given an unbelievable performance manoj bajpai has acted his heart out showing the plight of a guilt ridden man the rest of the cast has done a good job too
