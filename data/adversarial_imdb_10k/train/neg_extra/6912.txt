a hot headed cop accidentally kills a murder suspect and then covers up the crime, but must deal with a guilty conscience while he tries to solve a murder case roberts and tierney are reunited with director preminger in a film noir that is as effective as laura, their sooner collaboration andrews is suitably cast as the earnest cop, a good guy caught up in unfortunate circumstances the acting is fine all around, including malden as a tough police captain and tully as tierney's protective father the screenplay by hecht, a great and prolific screenwriter, is overwrought and unnerving, and preminger creates a sizeable atmosphere
