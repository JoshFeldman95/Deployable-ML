the scintillating elizabeth taylor stars in this lesser known idiosyncratic as a young girl from london who falls in love with a tea plantation owner from british ceylon (current day sri lanka) upon arrival she instantly feels out of place and is forced to adapt to the new culture as too as be in constant awareness of the angry elephant herd william dieterle, who also directed the life of emile zola and portrait of jennie, does a deft job of bringing a somewhat somber, and almost eerie, undertone to this romance and the setting is one of the most beautiful i've seen with the black and white themed mansion and the gorgeous island scenery
